Our Mission

We believe that every person deserves dignity, opportunity, and a path forward. JitaCorp combines cutting-edge business innovation with genuine social impact to create sustainable solutions for homelessness and disability.

  • Provide free housing to homeless and disabled individuals
  • Create meaningful employment and training opportunities
  • Build pathways to entrepreneurship through our incubator
  • Generate sustainable revenue for long-term impact
  • Scale to serve 800,000+ homeless and disabled Americans

The Hub Model

Each Hub serves 100 residents with integrated housing, employment, training, and entrepreneurship programs. We're building 8,000 Hubs for homeless individuals and 12,000 Hubs for the disabled population over the next decade.

How We Generate Impact

🏠

Section 8 Housing

We provide free housing to residents while collecting Section 8 rental subsidies from the government.

💰

Tax Credits & Leases

We extract tax credits from the Big Beautiful Bill and convert them to upfront cash and 15-year free leases.

👥

Work Opportunity Credits

We collect Work Opportunity tax credits for hiring homeless and disabled individuals for employment.

📈

Ticket to Work Program

We collect $37,000 per disability person in milestone payments as we transition them into the workforce.

🎓

Training Academy

We provide 20-hour/week job skills training and collect tax credits to fund the program.

🚀

Startup Incubator

We provide seed equity financing and test business models, spinning off profitable ventures and graduating participants.

City Cost Savings

The average homeless person costs cities $35,000 annually. We charge 50% the first year, then 25% for four years—saving cities money while providing comprehensive support.
